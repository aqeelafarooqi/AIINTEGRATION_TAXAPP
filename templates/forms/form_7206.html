Part I - Self-Employed Health Insurance Deduction
1 Enter the total amount of health insurance premiums you paid for coverage during the tax year for yourself, your spouse, and your dependents 1
2 Net profit and any other earned income from the business under which the insurance plan is established (from Schedule C, line 31; Schedule F, line 34; or Schedule K-1) 2
3 Enter the total of all qualified long-term care insurance premiums included on line 1 3
4 Long-term care insurance premiums limitation based on your age (see instructions) 4
5 Subtract line 4 from line 3. If zero or less, enter -0- 5
6 Subtract line 5 from line 1. If zero or less, enter -0- 6
